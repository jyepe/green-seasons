# Privacy Policy for Green Seasons

**Effective date:** May 4, 2026
**Last updated:** May 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Green Seasons mobile application ("Green Seasons", the "App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the App. Green Seasons is a wholesale produce ordering platform that connects restaurants with their delivery operator. By creating an account or using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. Who this policy applies to

Green Seasons has three categories of users:

- **Restaurant owners** — place orders for produce delivery to their restaurant.
- **Employees** — operational staff who view orders and prepare truck loads.
- **Administrators** — internal staff who manage the catalog, pricing, and accounts.

This policy applies to all of them.

---

## 2. Information we collect

We only collect information needed to operate the App. We do **not** use third-party advertising networks, analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, or tracking frameworks.

### 2.1 Information you provide directly

When you create an account or use the App, you provide:

| Data type                  | Examples                                                                       | Why we collect it                                                                                              |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Account identifiers**    | Email address                                                                  | To create your account, sign you in via magic link, and send transactional email (e.g. account confirmation).  |
| **Personal details**       | First name, last name, phone number                                            | To identify you within the App, contact you about orders, and display your name to staff handling your orders. |
| **Restaurant information** | Restaurant name, street address, city, postal code, country                    | To create a delivery destination for your orders.                                                              |
| **Order data**             | Items added to cart, quantities, delivery dates, order history, favorite items | To process and fulfill your orders and to show you order history and favorites.                                |
| **Role assignment**        | Whether your account is a restaurant owner, employee, or admin                 | To grant you the correct access within the App.                                                                |

### 2.2 Information generated by your use of the App

| Data type                   | Examples                                                                                                                                                          |
| --------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Authentication metadata** | Sign-in timestamps, session tokens (managed by Supabase Auth and stored locally on your device using encrypted storage).                                          |
| **Email send logs**         | A record indicating whether the welcome email and order confirmation emails were sent (no email _content_ is stored beyond what is required to know it was sent). |
| **Timestamps**              | When records (profile, orders, cart items, etc.) were created or last updated.                                                                                    |

### 2.3 Information we do **not** collect

We want to be explicit. The App does **not** collect:

- Precise or approximate **location** (no GPS, no IP-based geolocation by us).
- **Photos, camera, or microphone** data.
- **Contacts**, calendar, SMS, call logs, or files on your device.
- **Advertising identifiers** (AAID / IDFA).
- **Device fingerprints**, behavioral analytics, or usage telemetry.
- **Payment card numbers, bank accounts, or other financial credentials.** Green Seasons currently supports **cash-only** payments handled offline at delivery; no payment instrument is processed in-app.
- **Health, biometric, sexual orientation, religious, political, or other sensitive personal information.**

### 2.4 Children

Green Seasons is a B2B tool intended for restaurant operators and delivery staff. It is **not directed to children under 13** (or under 16 in jurisdictions where that is the relevant threshold). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

## 3. How we use your information

We use the information described in Section 2 only to:

1. Create and authenticate your account.
2. Process and fulfill your produce orders.
3. Display your cart, order history, favorites, and restaurant information back to you.
4. Allow employees and admins to fulfill, deliver, and finalize pricing for your orders.
5. Send you transactional emails (sign-in magic links, order confirmations, account welcome).
6. Maintain the security and integrity of the App (e.g. detect and prevent unauthorized access).
7. Comply with legal obligations.

We do **not** use your information for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

## 7. How long we keep your data

| Data                                 | Retention                                                                                                                                            |
| ------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Account profile (name, phone, email) | Until you delete your account.                                                                                                                       |
| Order history                        | Retained for as long as your account exists, and may be retained in anonymized or aggregated form afterward for legal, tax, and operational records. |
| Authentication session tokens        | Until they expire or you sign out. Stored locally on your device in secure storage.                                                                  |
| Email send logs                      | For the operational lifetime of the account.                                                                                                         |

When you delete your account (Section 8), we permanently remove your sign-in credentials, your profile (name, phone), your cart, and your saved favorites. Past orders are kept on file, but they are detached from you, so they no longer identify you personally. We keep these de-identified order records so we can maintain accurate financial and inventory history, as required by law.

## 8. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights:

- **Access** — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
- **Correction** — update inaccurate information directly in the App (Profile screen) or by contacting us.
- **Deletion** — delete your account directly from the App (Profile → Delete Account). This calls our `delete-account` function, which removes your account from authentication and cascades to your profile, cart, and favorites.
- **Portability** — request a machine-readable copy of your data.
- **Objection / restriction** — object to or restrict certain processing (where applicable under GDPR/UK GDPR).
- **Withdraw consent** — where processing is based on consent.
- **Lodge a complaint** — with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using Section 12. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

**California residents (CCPA/CPRA):** you have rights to know, delete, correct, and opt out of "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. Green Seasons does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

## 9. Security

We take security seriously:

- Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth using industry-standard tokens. Sessions are stored in encrypted local storage on your device.
- All network traffic between the App and our backend uses HTTPS/TLS.
- Database access is gated by Postgres **Row-Level Security (RLS)** so users can read and write only the data they are authorized to see.
- Administrative actions are restricted to an explicit allowlist of admin accounts.
- Backend secrets (service-role keys) are never embedded in the App.
